Baked Chicken Breast

  • Time: Couple Hours
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
  • Boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Butter or olive oil
  • Kosher Salt
  • Seasonings
Four pieces of chicken breasts

Steps:

  1. Brine
  2. Brush
  3. Rub
  4. Roast
  5. Rest

Toast

  • Time: 15 minutes or less
  • Serves: 1-2 people
Ingredients Quantity
  • Bread
1 or 2 pieces of toast

Steps:

  1. Get out bread
  2. Put bread in toaster
  3. Set timer if necessary
  4. Push down on handle
  5. Wait

Buttered Toast

  • Time: 15 minutes or less
  • Serves: 1-2 people
Ingredients Quantity
  • Bread
  • Butter
1 or 2 pieces of toast

Steps:

  1. Get out bread
  2. Put bread in toaster
  3. Set timer if necessary
  4. Push down on handle
  5. Wait
  6. Get out butter
  7. Get out knife
  8. Spread butter on toast using knife
